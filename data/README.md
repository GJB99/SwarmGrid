# SwarmGrid-Edge — Demo Video

Place a warehouse forklift dashcam video here named `demo_dashcam.mp4`.

## Recommended Sources
- Search YouTube for "forklift dashcam warehouse" or "forklift POV warehouse"
- Download a stock video from Pexels or Pixabay (royalty-free)

## Requirements
- Format: .mp4 (H.264 codec preferred)
- Resolution: 720p or 1080p
- Content: First-person forklift driving through a warehouse aisle

The server will loop this video continuously to simulate a live camera feed.
